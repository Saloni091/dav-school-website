Admission Process

Admission to D.A.V Public School is a simple and transparent process. Our aim is to make the admission process easy and stress-free for both students and parents. Please find the detailed steps below for applying to our school:

Eligibility Criteria

For admission to different classes, the student must meet the eligibility criteria set by the school. These criteria include age requirements, academic performance in previous institutions, and special consideration for siblings of existing students.

Documents Required

To complete the admission process, the following documents must be submitted:

  • Birth Certificate (for new admissions)
  • Previous Academic Records
  • Transfer Certificate (if applicable)
  • Recent Passport-sized Photographs
  • Aadhar Card (for Indian students)

Academics at D.A.V Public School Ranchi

At D.A.V Public School, we provide a comprehensive academic experience designed to foster intellectual growth and personal development in students. Our curriculum is designed to cater to the diverse learning needs and abilities of our students.

Curriculum

We follow the CBSE curriculum, ensuring a balance of academic rigor and holistic development. Our curriculum includes subjects in Science, Mathematics, Social Science, Languages, and the Arts. We also offer extracurricular activities to enhance creativity and teamwork.

Syllabus

The syllabus for Classes 1 to 5 includes fundamental concepts in subjects like English, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, and Environmental Education.

The syllabus for Classes 6 to 8 includes more advanced topics in Science, Mathematics, English, Social Studies, and additional languages.

The syllabus for Classes 9 to 12 is designed to prepare students for board exams. It includes in-depth subjects with a focus on Science, Commerce, and Humanities streams.

Faculty

Our faculty members are highly qualified and dedicated to providing the best educational experience for our students. They are trained to support the individual learning needs of each student and promote academic excellence.
